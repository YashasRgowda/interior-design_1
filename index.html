Scene 03 — The Method

Every commission moves through five acts — from the first conversation to the last object placed.

I

Listening

We begin with how you live, not what you own. Light, ritual, the rooms you actually inhabit — the brief is written from your day.

Discovery
II

Composition

Plans, proportion and flow. We move walls and sightlines before a single finish is chosen, so the space breathes correctly.

Architecture
III

Material

Sourced, not specified. Stone, timber, plaster and metal selected by hand — and by how they age into the room over years.

Palette
IV

Light

Our signature act. Natural and layered light is engineered so each room performs from dawn to the last lamp at night.

Atmosphere
V

Delivery

Styled to completion. We hand over a finished world — down to the books on the shelf and the scent in the hall.

Before we begin

Most residential projects unfold over 10 to 18 months — from the first listening session to the final styling walk-through. Larger residences or projects with significant architectural modification may extend to 24 months. We never rush a commission.

Yes. While our studios are in Dubai, Abu Dhabi and Mumbai, we have delivered projects across the GCC, Southeast Asia and Southern Europe. The commission simply needs to be one we can fully dedicate ourselves to.

Our design fees begin at AED 350,000 for smaller residences. Total project budgets — including materials, furniture and artisanal fabrication — vary widely depending on scope and specification. We discuss investment openly from the very first meeting.

Absolutely. We regularly collaborate with architects, structural engineers and specialist contractors. When the existing team shares our standard of craft, the results are stronger for it.

We source directly from quarries, mills, workshops and artisans — in Italy, India, Japan, Portugal and beyond. Every material is selected by hand and approved for how it will age and patina in your specific climate and light conditions.
